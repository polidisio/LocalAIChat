# LLMBox - App Store Text

## App Name
LLMBox

## Subtitle
Your Local AI Chat Interface

## Description
LLMBox is a chat interface to connect with local AI models like Ollama, Gemma, Qwen, and more.

**Features:**
- Connect to local AI servers on your network
- Interactive chat with real-time streaming responses
- Persistent conversation history
- Flexible switching between different AI models
- Modern interface inspired by Apple Messages
- Built-in model selector

**Perfect for:**
- Developers working with local LLMs
- Privacy-conscious users (data never leaves your network)
- Businesses with local AI infrastructure
